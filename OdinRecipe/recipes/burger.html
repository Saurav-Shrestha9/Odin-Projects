Burger

Momo are bite-size dumplings made with a spoonful of stuffing wrapped in dough. Momo are usually steamed, though they are sometimes fried or steam-fried.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Oil
  • Flour
  • Onions
  • Cabbage
  • Chicken/Buff/Veges

Steps

  1. Break your dough into small piece and dip those pieces in flour.
  2. Take the little pieces of dough and roll it to make a circle having a radius of about 5-7cm.
  3. Mince the vegetables/meat and mix all the fillings as per your liking.
  4. Pack the fillings by covering it with the dough.
  5. Fill the bottom of steamer with water, oil the carier and place each Mo:Mo making a little gap.
  6. Steam it for about 20-25 minutes.
  7. Your Mo:Mo's are ready to be eaten!!!
